Hello there, I’m Booshan!

I’m currently a Digital IC Design Engineer at MaxLinear in the SoC Design Team in Chennai, India.

Previously, I worked as a Mixed-Signal Verification Engineer at Ixana, a deep-tech startup which aims to revolutionize human-computer interaction! The company was in super-stealth mode until November 2022.

I obtained a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from CEG, Anna University. I consider it an inexplicable privilege to say that I spent most of my undergrad time at the Integrated Systems Lab (ISL) under Prof. P. V. Ramakrishna and Dr. J. Dhurga Devi.
